# 🌟 Fusion Development with SAP Cloud ERP and SAP Business Technology Platform

> Fusion Development brings together SAP Cloud ERP with SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP) through SAP Build Tools for seamless innovation across build offerings.*

---

## 🧩 Introduction

Welcome to the **Fusion Development Story** — where **SAP Cloud ERP**, **SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP)** converge to enable collaborative, agile innovation.

In this journey, **Fusion Teams** — made up of business experts, citizen developers, and professional developers — work together to design and extend intelligent business processes.  
This repository demonstrates a **real-world-like implementation story** using extensibility, automation, and integration across **SAP Cloud ERP** and **SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP)**.

---

## 🚀 What Is Fusion Development?

**Fusion Development** combines the strengths of different personas:

| Role | Strength | Tools |
|------|-----------|-------|
| 🧠 Business Experts | Define processes and requirements | SAP Cloud ERP |
| 🧩 Low code/ No code developers | Build low-code automations and UIs | SAP Build Process Automation |
| 💻 Professional developers | Extend and integrate systems | ABAP Cloud for on-stack developer extensibility, key user extensibility and side-by-side extensibility |

Together, they deliver **Clean-Core-compliant**, **upgrade-safe**, **event-driven**, and **composable** business extensions — driving agility across the enterprise.
